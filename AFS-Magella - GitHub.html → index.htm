Imports & Exports Clearing Services

  • Please Attach the Following For Your Imports:
  • Commercial Invoice;
  • Bill of Lading or Airway Bill;
  • Certificate of Origin;
  • Packing List;
  • Import Declaration Form;
  • Proof of Payment (e.g., receipt or bank slip);
  • Relevant Licenses or Permits (if applicable);
  • Insurance Certificate (if insured).

    • The faster you send these documents the faster we can speed up the clearing process.

  • Please Attach the Following For Your Exports
    • :
  • Obtain an export permit or license (if required);
  • Comply with export regulations and laws;
  • Declare the goods being exported;
  • Pay any applicable export duties or taxes;
  • Obtain a customs clearance document (e.g., export declaration).

    • The faster you send these documents the faster we can speed up the clearing process.
